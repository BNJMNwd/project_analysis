Project Analysis

Site Intent

Client Goals:

The goal of the client is to have a site that draws visitors to there unique interactive experience featuring exotic animals. They offer birthday parties, events and field trips. They want attract these visitors so they are able to support the animals that they currently have as well as to be able to bring in additional animals to add to there growing animal family. They also want to encourage site visitors to make a donation to support the animals and the habitats.
The animals have been featured on several TV shows and the client wishes to continue doing so. Because of this it is important for their site to showcase this information.
Another goal of the client is to showcase these amazing and unique animals to build love and support around the world.

User Goals:

The goals of the user are to have quick access to photos of the unique animals. They also want to be able to see what option they have to visit these animals.
They want information on the tours and events that are available to them.
